◆ FAQ

Questions We Hear Most.

Straight answers about projects, payments, and our refund guarantee.

The Deposit (43%) is the initial payment that confirms your project and allows development to begin. For a Ksh 60,000 project, your Deposit (43%): Ksh 25,800. The remaining balance is paid in milestones as each stage is delivered and approved by you.
If our team is unable to deliver your agreed project scope, you receive a full 110% refund guarantee — 110% of all payments made to date. This applies when a project cannot proceed due to reasons on our side. Full terms are included in your project agreement at sign-up.
All payments are made in Kenyan Shillings via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or other approved methods. Client payments are held securely and released to developers only upon successful milestone approval. No funds move until you confirm delivery of each stage.
Timelines depend on scope. A simple website may take 2–4 weeks. A mobile app or custom SaaS platform typically takes 6–16 weeks. Your project agreement includes a clear milestone schedule so you always know what to expect.
The free prototype is a limited-scope interactive demo covering core screens, layouts, and user flow. It is not a fully functional build but allows you to approve direction before full development investment. One free prototype per new paid project.
Free first-year hosting covers shared hosting for most websites and small web apps. Free domain covers a standard .co.ke or .com registration. Projects requiring dedicated hosting, premium domains, or extended maintenance are discussed at scoping.
Commissioners are client-facing sales partners who find clients, explain scope, and facilitate developer introductions. They earn 25–30% commission on every confirmed project and a 5% referral override when they recruit new commissioners. Payouts are released progressively at milestones.
Disputes are handled through our structured resolution process. Both client and developer submit their position. Our team reviews milestone records, communication logs, and deliverables. Where delivery has failed on our side, the 110% refund guarantee applies. We aim to resolve all disputes within 10 business days.
